GDT Video and photo curator

What is GDT?
As part of my bachelor thesis I was selected to be one of the two curators of the photographic and video project for Graphic Days Torino, the biggest event dedicated to visual communica- tion in Italy. Our goal was to study how an event is documented to the public through modern tools and create a digital storytelling to narrate the events to the whole world.
Photos and videos
We managed to document every aspect of the four days of the events, streaming numerous moments and photographing and recording others. Beside taking pictures and interacting with artists and guests my job was to select and edit the best pictures with the use of previously custom-made presets.
Volounteers managment
As one of the two heads of the media team I was in charge to manage and educate the vol- unteers, this was accomplished with numer- ous meetings and the creation of simple tool- kits in which every videomaker and photogra- pher could find pieces of information like the correct way to capture an image or how to in- teract with a guest or an exhibition.
Social network storytelling
I worked closely with the social media team on creating different editorial plans, one for each platform used by the festival. Together we edited and uploaded the different material provided by the volunteers.
Engage the crowd
We ideated numerous ways to allow the visi- tors to share the contents of the festival with their followers, a few examples are the cre- ation of personalized AR filters or the availabil- ity of a custom photo boot.
