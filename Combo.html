Graphic designer at Combo

What is Combo?
Combo is a modern hostel chain where art meets a new idea of hospitality. During my time there I was one of the three main repre- sentatives of the marketing department. My duties regarded mainly the production and diffusion of graphic supports used by the four different locations based in Torino, Milano, Venezia, and Bologna.
Event making and advertising
As part of the marketing department, I helped with the creation and advertising of exhibi- tions, parties, and Dj sets. Talking with artists, musicians, and yoga instructors was only part of my job, I was also in charge of the making of the supports used to promote the events.
Social media managment
The making of an editorial plan and copywrit- ing were the main tasks I followed regarding the brand's social network. Combo is a place where everybody is welcomed, the same can be said for its accounts. We used a friendly and engaging tone of voice putting the spot- light on the beautiful spaces and the enjoyable events that take place every day.
Images production
In an always-changing environment like Combo a camera was essential to capture new plates, and friendly faces. I also usually helped during photo shooting where I decorated the different environments.
Simple and big tasks
Being the only graphic designer I was the sole responsible for the production of simple ele- ments like restaurant menus and merchandis- ing. In the meantime, I kept updating the already existing brand guidelines in order to keep up with the latest trends and innovations. The biggest task I was assigned was the com- plete renovation of the latest location's wayfin- ding, where I worked in collaboration with the external design studio “LeftLoft”.
